Creamy Garlic Pasta

Description

Creamy garlic pasta is a simple yet satisfying dish that combines the richness of a creamy sauce with the savory flavor of garlic. It's a quick and easy weeknight meal that can be customized with your favorite vegetables, proteins, or cheeses. The base of the dish is typically a combination of butter, olive oil, and minced garlic, which is cooked until fragrant. Then, heavy cream, milk, or broth is added to create a luscious sauce. The cooked pasta is tossed in the sauce, and often, grated Parmesan cheese is sprinkled on top for an extra layer of flavor.

This versatile dish can be adapted to suit various dietary needs and preferences. For a vegetarian option, add spinach, mushrooms, or sun-dried tomatoes. To make it more substantial, incorporate cooked chicken, shrimp, or bacon. Experiment with different types of pasta, such as spaghetti, penne, or fettuccine, to find your favorite. Creamy garlic pasta is a comforting and flavorful dish that is sure to please everyone at the table.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cups chicken broth, or more as needed
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1 ½ tablespoons dried parsley

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat olive oil in a medium pan over medium heat. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Add butter and stir constantly until melted.
  3. Pour in 3 cups chicken broth; add pepper and salt. Bring to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Add more chicken broth if pasta starts to stick to the pan.
  4. Add Parmesan cheese, cream, and parsley and mix until thoroughly combined. Serve immediately.
